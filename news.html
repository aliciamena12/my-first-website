HITMAN 3


Release Date: Jan 20, 2021.
Platforms: PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Stadia, Xbox One, Xbox Series X.
Developer: Io Interactive.
Genre(s): Action Adventure, General.

Summary: HITMAN 3 is the dramatic conclusion to the World of Assassination trilogy and takes players around the world on a globetrotting adventure to sprawling sandbox locations. Every mission location in HITMAN 3 is meticulously detailed and packed full of creative opportunities. Supported by his Agency handler, Diana Burnwood, 47 joins forces with his long-lost friend Lucas Grey. When the dust settles, 47 and the world he inhabits will never be the same again.
The World of Assassination trilogy is fully realised with HITMAN 3 as the ultimate place to play all three games. It will be possible to 'import' owned locations, unlocks and progression from the previous two HITMAN games, essentially putting all 20+ locations from the entire trilogy in one place.
